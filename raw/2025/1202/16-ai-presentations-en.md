## Key Tools

### Gamma (Top Pick)
- AI generates full decks/docs/websites in minutes
- Card-based, responsive design
- 100+ themes, animations, transitions
- Real-time collaboration
- Export: PPT, PDF, Google Slides
- View analytics
- Pricing: Free (limited credits) / Plus $8-10 / Pro $15-20 per month
- Rating: 3.9-5 stars across reviews

### Beautiful.ai
- Smart slides auto-adjust layouts
- Professional auto-design for non-designers
- Pro exports + analytics
- Pricing: Pro $12/mo, Team $40/user/mo
- Rating: 7.5/10 G2, 4.7/5 stars
- Limitations: rigid templates, no free plan

### Tome AI (⚠️ Discontinued)
- Narrative-driven with AI images/videos
- Web-style presentations
- Stopped presentation service April 2025
- Was: Free / Pro $10-16/mo

### Other Notable Tools
- **Pitch** — collaborative decks with AI blocks
- **SlidesAI** — Google Slides integration
- **MagicSlides** — text-to-slides in Google Slides
- **Presentations.ai** — enterprise AI presentations
