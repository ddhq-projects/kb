## Key Tools

### SEO
- Semrush — all-in-one, 50+ tools, keyword research, audits ($165/mo)
- Ahrefs — 36T backlink index, content gap analysis ($129/mo)
- Google Search Console — free, official Google data
- Surfer SEO — AI content optimization ($79/mo)

### Email Marketing
- ActiveCampaign — advanced automations, behavioral triggers ($15/mo)
- Mailchimp — multi-channel, free up to 500 contacts ($13/mo)
- Omnisend — eCommerce, pre-built segments ($16/mo)
- Klaviyo — eCommerce/B2C, email + SMS

### Analytics
- Google Analytics (GA4) — free, AI predictions, event tracking
- HubSpot Marketing Hub — CRM-connected full-funnel ($800/mo)
- Mixpanel — user behavior, event tracking ($28/mo)
- Hotjar — heatmaps, session recordings

### CRM
- HubSpot CRM — free core, marketing-sales alignment
- Salesforce — enterprise AI, scalability
- Zoho CRM — versatile AI, customization
- Klaviyo — eCommerce retention

## Recommended Stack
Semrush (SEO) + ActiveCampaign (email) + Google Analytics (insights) + HubSpot (CRM) → integrate via Zapier
