## Key Tools

### Task Management
- Todoist — natural language input, free + $5/mo
- Asana — team collaboration, free-form projects
- TickTick — reminders, scheduling, habits
- Motion — AI time optimization
- OmniFocus — robust personal task management
- Pocket Informant — tasks + calendar + notes + projects

### Note-Taking
- Notion — flexible workspace, second brain, AI
- Microsoft OneNote — free all-rounder, unlimited devices
- Evernote — AI features, cross-platform
- Obsidian — linked knowledge graphs, local storage
- Google Keep — simple, Google Workspace integration
- Notability / GoodNotes — iPad handwriting, PDF markup
