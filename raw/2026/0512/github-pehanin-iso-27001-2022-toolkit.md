# ISO 27001:2022 Toolkit — GitHub Repository

**License:** MIT
**Fetched:** 2026-05-12

This repository contains a comprehensive toolkit designed to help organizations implement the ISO 27001:2022 Information Security Management System (ISMS). The toolkit includes templates, policies, plans, and checklists that align with the ISO 27001:2022 standards.

## Toolkit Contents

1. **Gap Assessment Plan** — helps identify gaps between your organization's current information security controls and those required by ISO 27001:2022.

2. **Statement of Applicability (SoA)** — outlines which ISO 27001:2022 controls are applicable to your organization based on the risk assessment. Includes the implementation status of each control.

3. **Risk Register** — a dynamic tool to assess, document, and manage risks associated with your organization's information assets. Helps in identifying risks and developing mitigation strategies.

4. **Scope and Context Definition** — defines the boundaries and context of your ISMS, including internal and external factors that affect your ability to achieve ISMS objectives.

5. **Asset Inventory** — a comprehensive list of the organization's information assets, including their classification, location, ownership, criticality and control measures.

6. **Business Continuity and Disaster Recovery Plan** — a detailed plan for maintaining operations during and after a disaster. Includes step-by-step procedures.

7. **Information Security Policy and Procedures** — defines the overall approach to information security, including employee roles and responsibilities, access control, and data handling procedures.

8. **Awareness and Training Plan** — ensures employees are aware of their roles in maintaining security standards and receive regular training.

9. **Management Review Meeting** — documentation for recording discussions, decisions, and actions regarding ISO 27001 implementation.

10. **ISMS Checklists** — various checklists to help with ISMS implementation and organizational readiness.

11. **Internal Audit Plan** — outlines how the organization will audit the ISMS to ensure continued compliance and identify areas for improvement.

12. **Return on Investment (ROI) Analysis** — financial analysis evaluating costs versus benefits of ISO 27001 implementation.

## Key Features

- Comprehensive Templates: Ready-to-use templates for various aspects of ISMS implementation
- Compliance-Focused: Aligned with ISO 27001:2022 clauses
- Customizable: Easily adaptable to suit specific needs
- Structured Approach: Breaks down implementation into manageable steps
